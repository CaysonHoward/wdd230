Site Purpose:

The purpose of this site is to help increase awareness for the town of New Plymouth. The goal is to provide attention to the attractions that are in New Plymouth to inrease the number of people visiting and bring much needed life to the town.

Available Domain:

newplymouthcommerce.com

Target Audience:

Any person wanting to get a quick look at what's going on in New Plymouth and activity's they can join if they are in the area. This website is for those 16-100 and can attend community events

Example use cases:

Traveler going through the local area

Motivation to use the site: Wants to make a stop while they are traveling to try some new things

New Resident seeking to become a better community member

Motivation to use the site: Wants to start/join events that are happening in the area

Scenarios:

  1. What are some upcoming events in my area?
  2. Being new to the area, I want to make some new friends and meet new people.
  3. What are some popular things to do in my area?
  4. I want to learn what goes on behind the scenes in my community.
  5. How can I be more involved in the community?
